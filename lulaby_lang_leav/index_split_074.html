A Winter Song
She was the song,
in a chorus—unheard.
You were the summer
in her winter of verse.

 

Yours was the melody
she wanted to learn;
it clung to her lips,
in silence it yearned.

 

It seems as though now,
you forgot every word;
in a field full of flowers,
she was the first.

 

There once was a song
you reminded her of—
she no longer longs,
yet she still loves.
