When
When every dream
has turned to dust,
and your highest hopes
no longer soar.

 

When places you
once yearned to see,
grow further away
on distant shores.

 

When every night
you close your eyes,
and long inside
for something more.

 

Remember this
and only this,
if nothing else
you can recall—

 

There was a life
a girl once led,
where you were loved
the most of all.
